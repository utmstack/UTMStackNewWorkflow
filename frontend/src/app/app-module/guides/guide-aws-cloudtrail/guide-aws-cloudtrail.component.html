AWS Cloudwatch

For this integration to work properly, first you need to configure the IAM user for UTMStack.

  1. 1 In the CloudTrail panel, select “Create trail”.

  2. 2 Fill in the "Trail name" field.

  3. 3 Mark "Select all S3 buckets in your account".

  4. 4 Fill in the "S3 bucket" field. The name of the bucket must be unique in S3. Then click on “Create”.

  5. 5 Click on the name of the trail to edit.

  6. 6 Configure CloudWatch Logs.

  7. 7 Fill the group name and continue.

  8. 8 Click on “Allow” to grant CloudTrail permissions.

  9. 9 Click on the button shown below, to activate the UTMStack features related to this integration
