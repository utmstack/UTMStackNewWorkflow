Asset management

This integration enables the built-in UTMStack assets management feature. No third-party software is required to enable this integration.
Asset management facilitates the continuous identification of assets connected to the network using active and passive scanners to detect physical and virtual resources such as servers and switches. Get alerted when suspicious devices are connected to the network or behave in potentially dangerous ways.
Asset management is fully integrated with other features such as SIEM and Vulnerability management. Activity from multiple sources is correlated to identify potential threats.
