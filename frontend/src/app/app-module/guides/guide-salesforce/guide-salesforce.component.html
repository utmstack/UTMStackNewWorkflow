Salesforce

You can install webhooks on an organization or on a specific repository.

  1. 1 Go to "Setup" and select "Setup for the current app" option at the top right of the screen.

  2. 2 Type "App Manager" in the quick search box and select the "App Manager" option.

  3. 3 On the "Lightning Experience App Manager" screen, click on the "New Connected App" button and fill in the fields.

  4. 4 In the "API (Enable OAuth Settings)" section, check "Enable OAuth Settings" and use https://login.salesforce.com/services/oauth2/success as the "Callback URL" value.

  5. 5 In the same section, be sure to select "Access Connect REST API resources (chatter_api)" in the "Selected OAuth Scopes" field.

  6. 6 Save your changes and select "Continue" on the next screen.

    Changes can take up to 10 minutes to take effect.

  7. 7 In your Connected App go to the "API (Enable OAuth Settings)" section, click the "Manage Consumer Details" button next to "Consumer Key and Secret".

  8. 8 Once you verify your identity, you should see your customer key and secret in the "Consumer Details" section. Save your customer key and secret in a safe place using the "Copy" button.

  9. 9 Fill in the form with the data obtained in the previous steps.

    Client ID is required
    Client Secret is required
    Username is required
    Password is required
    Security Token is required
    Instance URL is required
